## The Rule

Every brand answers the same 5 questions:

1. What is the one thing this does?
2. Why does it make/save money?
3. Why can I trust it?
4. What do I do next?
5. What gets benched?

Anything that is cool but unclear, hard to explain, hard to sell, hard to trust, hard to onboard, or not needed for the main revenue path — **bench it**. Not killed. In the vault for later.

# Contributing to X407

Thank you for your interest.

## Contribution model

This repository is managed as a strategic commercial project. Public suggestions are welcome, but core protocol, monetization, compliance, and proprietary engine changes may be maintained under restricted contribution rules.

## Before contributing

Please open an issue describing:

- the problem
- the proposed change
- the business or technical value
- whether the idea touches protected IP areas

## Protected areas

The following areas may require explicit review before any contribution is considered:

- pricing logic
- anti-abuse and fraud controls
- compliance automation
- enterprise workflow logic
- settlement orchestration
- restricted roadmap items

## Quality expectations

- concise, structured proposals
- commercial and technical rationale
- diagrams where useful
- no sensitive credentials or customer information

## Legal note

Submission does not guarantee acceptance. Accepted work may require additional contributor terms or assignment provisions.

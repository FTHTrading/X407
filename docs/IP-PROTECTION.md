# X407 IP Protection Framework

## Objective

Protect the commercial moat while enabling market adoption.

## Protection layers

### 1. Brand protection
- protect X407 and related marks
- maintain consistent naming and logo usage
- publish brand guidelines separately

### 2. Copyright protection
- documentation
- diagrams
- SDK examples
- site copy
- strategic frameworks

### 3. Trade secret protection
Keep these outside broad public disclosure:
- pricing optimization models
- anti-fraud heuristics
- conversion tuning logic
- enterprise routing rules
- internal operational playbooks

### 4. Contractual controls
- NDAs for deeper disclosures
- customer access terms
- partner enablement agreements
- contributor IP assignment where required

### 5. Repository segmentation
Use multiple layers of repos and access:
- public narrative repo
- partner integration repo
- enterprise enablement repo
- internal moat repo

## Rule of thumb

Publish what accelerates adoption. Restrict what creates irreversible competitive advantage.
